About Us

Based out of Greenfield, MA, we're Small Town Radio—the band that decided the songs you know needed a little extra attitude. We're a group of local musicians who think that if you can't dance to it, it’s probably not worth playing. Whether it's classic rock anthems or modern pop bangers, we treat every song like a long-lost cousin who just crashed the party. We’ve been rocking stages around Greenfield and beyond, bringing that hometown spirit with a twist of electric chaos. We like loud guitars, good company, and occasionally getting the tempo just a *little* too fast. Come catch a show, grab a drink, and let's turn the volume up together.
