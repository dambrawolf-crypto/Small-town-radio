About Us

What happens when you take the classic rock you love and fuse it with the pop energy of today? You get Small town radio. Based out of Greenfield, MA, we pride ourselves on a unique approach to live music—taking songs you think you know by heart and giving them a fresh, rock-ified identity. Between our unique mashups and our own original compositions, we aim to bridge the gap between old-school grit and modern groove. We’re here to play the music you didn’t know you needed to hear, played the way it was meant to be felt: live, loud, and inspired.
